The power of consistency

If you have a good couple of days and establish momentum toward what you want, then fall back a few days, the next good days you are playing catch up to get to where you already were - emotionally, psychologically, or physically.

Everyone knows the story of the tortoise and the hare.

Consistent habits, and results that accumulate and compound day over day over day are very powerful in achieving results.

Are you consistent?
